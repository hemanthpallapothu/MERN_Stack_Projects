Our Roots

Built in 1966 by Francisco and Juanita Nepomuceno, the hotel was initially established to provide accommodations to American servicemen stationed at the nearby Clark Air Force Base
Since then, Oasis Hotel has stayed true to that same tradition of hospitality and is currently being run by the founders’ second and third-generation family members.
